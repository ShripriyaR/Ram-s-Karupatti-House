What We Do

We are a Group of Families Authentically Manufacturing Palm Products Along with the other Local Farmers.

Our mission is to Create a Sugar free Society, We are creating a Community of people to achieve our Vision. Join our Sugar-free community to get more quality products.

How We Do

Our Signature Product Palm Jaggery(கருப்பட்டி) is made by extracting the sap preserving all the natural goodness and nutritional benefits of the palm tree sap.

Then it will be stored with a Traditional way of making it Dry. Then it will be shared to the Quality checker & then it will be delivered to you with all the Love from the Palm Farms of Nambiyur,Erode District.
